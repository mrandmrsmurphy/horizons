### Johannine
John uses this verb 104 times.  Normally (38 times out of 104), John uses the preposition eis and an accusative case.  Only John 9:18 uses the preposition 'peri'. Only John 3:15 and 6:30 use the preposition 'ev'.  48/104 times there is no preposition.  23 times the verb is used bare, without object or preposition.  Twice it is used with two objects, in John 2:24 and 17:20.
