## Usage
### NT
- Fathers don't give snakes - Matthew 7:10 & Luke 11:11
- They're intelligent - Matthew 10:16
- The Pharisee's are - Matthew 23:33
- tread down ... and scorpions - Luke 10:19
- Moses' Brass Serpent - John 3:14
  - testing God in the wilderness 1 Corinthians 10:9
- The Garden on Eden reference - 2 Corinthians 11:3
- Description of the 4 horse/angels - trumpet 6 of Seven Trumpets of Judgments - Revelation 9:19
- The Dragon - figure 2 of the Seven Mystic Figures
  - Revelation 12:9, 14, 15 - Precisely paralleled in 20:2

; Apocryphal : *Mark 16:18* - probably an extrapolation of Luke 10:19
