## Usage
The only NT use is Colossians 2:5.  "steadfastness" seems to fit best.

### LXX
- The "firmament" of the Creation Story/Genesis 1 (6, 7, 8, 14, 15, 17 & 20). Also Psalm 19:1.
- The "firmament" over God at Mt. Sinai - Exodus 24:10 (perhaps also Deuteronomy 33:26?)
- The "firmament" over God in Ezekiel's vision (Ezekiel 1:22, 23 & 25; 10:1)
- "steadfastness" - Ezekiel 13:5,  Psalm 18:2, Psalm 71:3, Psalm 73:4, Psalm 150:1, Esther 9:29
