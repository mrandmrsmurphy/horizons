preposition using Genitive or Dative

- With Genitive
*# Representation/Advantage: on behalf of, for the sake of
*# Reference/Respect: concerning, with reference to (= περι)
*# Substitution: in the place of, instead of (= αντι)
*#* (such instances also involve representation)
- With Accusative
*# Spatial: over, above
*# Comparison: more than, beyond

## Pauline (99)
### Colossians
1. Colossians 1:7 - G - ὅς ἐστιν πιστὸς ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν διάκονος - (Epaphrus) us a faithful minister **on your behalf**
1. Colossians 1:9 - G - οὐ παυόμεθα ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν προσευχόμενοι - we do not cease praying **for you**

1. Colossians 1:24b - G - ἐν τῇ σαρκί μου ὑπὲρ τοῦ σώματος αὐτοῦ - in my flesh **for his (Christ's) body's sake**
1. Colossians 2:1 - G - ἡλίκον ἀγῶνα ἔχω ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν - How great a conflict I have **for you**
1. Colossians 4:12 - G - πάντοτε ἀγωνιζόμενος ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν ἐν ταῖς προσευχαῖς - (Epaphrus) is always striving **for you** in prayer
1. Colossians 4:13 - G - ἔχει πολὺν πόνον ὑπὲρ ὑμῶν - (Epaphrus) has great zeal **for you**
