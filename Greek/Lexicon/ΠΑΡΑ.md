Preposition taking the Genitive, Dative, or Accusative

1. With Genitive: in general, from (the side of) (with a personal object)
## Source/Spatial: from
## Agency: from, by
1. With Dative: in general, proximit or nearness
## Spatial: near, beside
## Sphere: in the sight of, before (someone)
## Association: with (someone/something)
## Virtually equivalent to simple dative
1. With Accusative
1. Spatial: by, alongside of, near, on
## Comparison: in comparison to, more than
## Opposition: against, contrary to
