"When Paul uses the verb 'await' (**ἀπεκδέχομαι**), he always describes future eschatological expectations (Rom 8:19,23,25; 1Cor 1:7; Phil 3:20).  He anticipates a time when believer's righteousness will be brought to its crowning conclusion at the end of this age, when the character transformation that has already begun is completed." - Don Garlington, *A Faith That is Never Alone*, p.304-305
