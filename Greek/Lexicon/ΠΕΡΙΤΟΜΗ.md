### "Of the circumcision"

1. Romans 4:12 - GSF - τοῖς οὐκ ἐκ περιτομῆς μόνον - not to those of circumcision only
1. Titus 1:10 - GSF - μάλιστα οἱ ἐκ τῆς περιτομῆς, - especially those of the circumcision
The Romans text seems to point the origin of the phrase: it refers to physical Israel, the Jews.  Abraham is the progenitor of both his genetic descendants, and the spiritual believers in the Lord (Abraham believed before he was circumcised).
