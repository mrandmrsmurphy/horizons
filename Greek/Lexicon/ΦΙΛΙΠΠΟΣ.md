1. Philip Herod tetrarch of Trachonitis, was brother to Herod Antipas, by the father's, but not by the mother's side. Philip was born of Cleopatra, of Jerusalem, and Herod of Malthace, a Samaritan: he died in the twentieth year of Tiberias, five years after his mention in Lk 3:1. He built Caesarea Philippi. His step brother Herod Antipas, married his wife unlawfully.
