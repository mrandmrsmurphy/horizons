### LXX
- Numbers 21:5 for קְלֹקֵל, DSM modifying bread (ἄρτος meaning "thin" or "light".

### More
Modern LXX texts have this word -- in place of the two parts (διά + κενος) -- in:
- Job 2:3
- Psalm 31:6
- Psalm 25:3
- 1 Maccabees 6:12
- Leviticus 26:16
- Proverbs 23:29
- Sirach 23:11
