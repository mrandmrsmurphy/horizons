## Usage
### Matthean
This is a structurally significant word in Matthew.   There is an earthquake and them people come out of tombs in chapter 8, 27 and 28.  Chapter 23 reveals that the Jewish leaders of Jesus' day -- the Scribes and the Pharisees -- will not be those raised from tombs because they ***build***' them.  They remain in their graves.
