## Usage
### NT
1. Luke 8:4 (PMP/GPM) - used in an active sense, in a Genitive Absolute, of a crowd journeying out to Jesus from the cities.
### LXX
1. Leviticus 26:33 (PMP/NSF) - used in active sense, attributive of a sword (ΜΑΧΑΙΡΑ), journeying after God's people.
1. Ezekiel 39:14 (PMP/APM) - used in the active sense, attendant circumstances, of men (ΑΝΗΡ) journeying through the land.
