Waw is a Hebrew letter with a numerical value of six.

- As a prefix to a noun, Waw functions as a conjunction, meaning *and* or *but*.

- As a prefix on a verb, Waw reverses the tense, turning future into past or past into future.

- As a suffix, Waw mean "your" in the Masculine Singular - [[Hebrew:וֹ]
