According to one scholar, to "the ability to grasp the meanings of implications of a situation or message, the ability to understand practical matters and interpersonal relations and make beneficial decisions."  It refers not merely to an ability to reason and formulate arguments, but to a kind of intuition about situations, persons, and how best to handle them.
