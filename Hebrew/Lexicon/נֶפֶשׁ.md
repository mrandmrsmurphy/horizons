[[Soul]
**נפש** has many cognates in the Semitic languages, most importantly Akkadian, Arabic and Ugarit, where there is comparible variety of meaning, yet the basic identity of the root meaning is "breath".  Other ANE meanings include, "life, person, self, and desire" of which Hebrew also avails itself.  In rare instances (e.g. Psalm 69:1 and Jonah 2:5) and Akkadian rendering "throat" may be found in the OT, but most of the examples are adequately rendered alternatively.
