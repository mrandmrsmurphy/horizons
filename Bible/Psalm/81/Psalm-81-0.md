#### Phrases
- **instrument of Gath** — this one word in Hebrew ([[Help:Hebrew:גתית|גִּתִּית]]/''gittith'') eludes us to this day.  It is related to the place/people name 'Gath' and the noun for 'winepress'.  It is assumed to be a musical instrumental, but we cannot be sure.
